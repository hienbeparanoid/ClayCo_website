SALES POLICY

Beaurity provides nationwide delivery service through its website, Fanpage, and hotline. Orders are dispatched to customer addresses through a third-party courier, identified by a branded logo on the invoice attached to the package.

I. Pricing Policy

The listed product prices on the website include taxes, packaging fees, and other associated costs, excluding additional fees confirmed via hotline or email during order processing.

II. Delivery Time

  • Inner-city Ho Chi Minh City: Delivery within 2-3 days after ordering.
  • Suburban Ho Chi Minh City and other provinces: 2-5 days for central city areas and 5-6 days for remote areas.
  • In cases of delivery delays due to holidays, remote locations, or third-party courier issues, we appreciate customer understanding. Beaurity will notify and support customers in such cases.

PAYMENT POLICY

Customers can choose one of the following payment methods:
    I. Cash on Delivery (COD):
  • Step 1: Review product information on the website
  • Step 2: Order online, selecting Cash on Delivery
  • Step 3: Beaurity confirms recipient details
  • Step 4: Beaurity dispatches the order to the courier
  • Step 5: Recipient receives, inspects, and pays
    II. Bank Transfer:
  • Step 1: Review product information on the website
  • Step 2: Order online, selecting Bank Transfer
  • Step 3: Complete bank transfer following third-party instructions
  • Step 4: Beaurity confirms recipient details
  • Step 5: Recipient receives and inspects the order
    III. Momo Wallet Payment:
  • Step 1: Review product information on the website
  • Step 2: Order online, selecting Momo Wallet Payment
  • Step 3: Complete payment following Momo's instructions
  • Step 4: Beaurity confirms recipient details
  • Step 5: Recipient receives and inspects the order

RETURN AND WARRANTY POLICY

Product handling and return policy

  • If customers are unavailable during delivery, the courier will attempt redelivery (up to 2 attempts). If unsuccessful, Beaurity will refund the order within 15 business days.
  • For undelivered items due to unreachable customers within 3 days, Beaurity will return the product and notify customers of the canceled order.
